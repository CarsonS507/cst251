Challange

For this lab we were tasked with creating multiple div ids with the tags:floated, center-horiz, center-img, and center-both. We also had to link back to our home index and the CSS file for this page. All images had to be around 100 to 150 px wide. Divs had to be 30% wide. The center-horz centered the text, center-img centered the img horizontally, and center-both centers the image both vertically and horizontally.
