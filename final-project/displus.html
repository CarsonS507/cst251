What is Disney Plus?

Launched on November 12th, 2019, Disney Plus is a subscription based video on-demand streaming service run by the Walt Disney Company that offers content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic. This content comes in the form of films and television series produced by the Walt Disney Company. A subscription to Disney Plus costs either $6.99 per month or $69.99 a year.

How Does Disney Plus Work?

Disney Plus allows you to stream on up to four devices at once, whether it's a TV, computer, mobile phone/ tablet, or a gaming console. You can have multiple profiles per account allowimg you to have seperate watch lists between the people leeching off of your account. Once you select your profile you are greeted with Disney Plus's home screen. At the top of the home screen is a large banner that transitions between pictures of new and popular content on the service. Below the large banner is five grey buttons with white text labled Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic. When you hover over one of the buttons a GIF plays in the grey space of the button. Selecting the button will take you to a new page that contains content specific to the category you selected. Below these five buttons is a scrolling list of content recommended for you based on what you watch. The icons on this list and the ones under it are buttons showing an image of the show/movie along with it's title. Under the recommended list is one for things new to Disney Plus and one for content you started watching already. After these two lists the rest that appear on the home screen reflects what the individual user watches.

On the left side of the homepage is a sidebar that contains a searchbar, a return to home button, a button to go to your watchlist, a button that will lead you to a page specifically for movies, a button for a list of series, a button for Disney Plus original content, and a settings menu at the very bottom.

Format wise the entire site is very organized and has a dark blue semi gradient background with white text. All of the buttons for content are the same size rectangles with rounded corners and a dark dropshadow effect. When you select one of the content buttons it will take you to a page that provides a short summary of the show or movie along with a run time, rating, genre, and year it was released.
